# Iron Man — Mission Log: Story 1

Tony Stark picked up an anomalous energy signature 340 miles off the
coast of Norway using FRIDAY's deep scan array. He cross-referenced it
against three SHIELD satellite feeds and confirmed it as a dormant
HYDRA submarine base in 6.1 seconds.

He dove solo in the Mark L suit, reaching the underwater facility at
2,300 feet depth. The outer hull had a reinforced electromagnetic lock
— he bypassed it by generating a counter-frequency pulse from his left
repulsor, calibrated on the fly in 2.8 seconds.

Inside, he located the HYDRA mainframe and jacked in directly. FRIDAY
cracked the encryption in 19 seconds, pulling 4.7TB of operational
data including active sleeper agent locations across 11 countries.

Before exiting, he planted a miniature arc-powered charge on the
server core, set to a 4-minute delayed wipe to cover their tracks.
He was out and 600 feet clear before it triggered.

Total time inside the facility: 7 minutes 42 seconds.
